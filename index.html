What could a tiered EU become?

A multi-speed Europe could let willing states integrate faster while keeping a clear path for candidates, neighbours, and democratic friends to move closer to the European project.

  • Open doors — associate membership can make progress tangible before full accession.
  • Shared standards — closer tiers should still orbit the same democratic and legal values.
  • Strategic weight — security, energy, climate, and crisis response can reach beyond Europe.
  • Forward motion — countries can move inward as capacity, trust, and public support grow.
