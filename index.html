What could a tiered EU become?

A multi-speed Europe could let willing states integrate faster while keeping a clear path for candidates, neighbours, and democratic friends to move closer to the European project.

Integration as a ladder, not a wall

The outer tier keeps the democratic community visible; the inner tier shows where deeper defence, eurozone, Schengen, and foreign policy integration could move first.
